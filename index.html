Techno Cultural Club

With a motive to provide a common platform for Interdisciplinary technical activities of the college, Technical Club was launched in 2017. It aims to technically strengthen the students by integrating their skills in the various fields of Engineering & Technology, so as to cope up with the highly competitive environment. Many activities are conducted by the club in order to help the students explore and learn some skills which might help them in future. The club also aims competitions that are organized by other colleges, in which the students have performed exceptionally well.


About Us

Techno Cultural Club aspire students to ideate future technologies and make them come true. We promote the culture of hands-on skill that develop vision and allows student to learn in community. The community allows students to not only enhance technical skills in various field like cloud computing, drone technology, artificial intelligence & machine learning, web development, application development for various platforms, cyber security, etc. but to also learn soft skills such as team lead and team effort by promoting participation in various hackathon and events. The vision of the club is to unite technologies to serve the community. The only criteria we see is the enthusiasm and ambition of the students to learn and develop new products and technology. To create a learning environment we allow senior members to help, guide and mentor the new students.
